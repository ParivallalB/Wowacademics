ACCA Exemptions

ACCA offers exemptions to students who have studied relevant accountancy qualifications prior to starting ACCA’s accountancy qualifications. Exemptions stop you from having to study the same topics that you have already been taught. This is great if you have recently achieved a qualification, as it’s still fresh in your mind.
