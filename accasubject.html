Professional Level

Strategic Business Reporting
  • To prepare the financial statements of groups of entities
  • To interpret and advise on the meaning of financial statements
  • To communicate the impact of changes in accounting regulation on financial reporting
  • Theory - 40%
  • Pass - 51% (For Earlier P2)
SBR
Strategic Business Leader
  • To demonstrate organisational leadership & senior advisory capabilities & relevant professional skills
  • Technical areas are: Leadership, governance, strategy, risk, data analytics, organisational control, finance, change management.
  • Theory - 100%
  • Pass - 49% (For Earlier P3)
